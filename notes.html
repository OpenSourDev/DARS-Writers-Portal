Notes

Sales contract by third party representation

If an individual comes to sell any movable or immovable property on behalf of a third party, the person who originally represented the person and the third party representative are the ones who are mentioned in the sale.

Rental contract on electronic map

When one or more individuals who have an electronic map and bring a color printout or copy of it to sign a lease agreement, they cannot sign the agreement for more than 3 years.

About Evidences for a loan contract

When any individual or organization comes to sign a loan agreement with collateral, they are required to provide two witnesses. However, if the loan agreement is unsecured, no witnesses are required.

About loan contract and marital status

Any individual lender and borrower (not including companies!) when entering into any type of loan agreement must provide proof of marriage for both parties, i.e. a marriage certificate if married and a certificate of celibacy if unmarried.

Full representation through marriage certificate

If a husband or wife comes with their marriage certificate and a license in the name of the wife or husband and says they want to give full power of attorney to my wife or husband regarding the car, it is only a direct family power of attorney.

About fourth party representation

When a previously delegated person comes to transfer their delegation to a third party, they cannot select to transfer their delegation to a third party from the system!! Because it is not possible to transfer delegation to a fourth party.

Representation about a car purchased through a microfinance loan

When an individual comes to represent a car purchased with a loan from any microfinance institution and leased, in addition to the letter showing that the microfinance institution is holding the car, a document/letter showing that the microfinance institution is registered with the National Bank must be submitted.

Delegation of authority to manage a previously opened bank account

When submitting a letter from a bank to authorize a representative to make income and expenses on an already opened bank account, they must submit a bankbook or passbook along with it. If they do not already have a bankbook, it should be written on the letter as "without a passbook." If they do not have a passbook for any other reason, a photo of the passbook should be attached to the letter.

Sales contract through third party representation

If an individual comes to sell any movable or immovable property with a representation of a third party, when we enter the sale in the system, the individual who gave the representation and the third party are the representative. The interlocutor/delegate is not mentioned.

Car sales contract husband or wife relationship on the representation system

If a husband has full power of attorney for his wife or a wife has full power of attorney for her husband in a car sale, the person who brought the license may be named as the representative in the power of attorney.

Short notes

📢 When working on the customs transit representation system, it is required to be on administrative representation.

📢 On car sales: If the license plate code is 03, it must have a road transport clearance

📢 If it is mandatory for a bank to sign a loan agreement on representation, it must be on special representation; it cannot be on administrative representation.

For not giving an existing bank account with a valid authorization

Even if you receive a letter from the bank regarding the management of an existing bank account when making a delegation, the check box for making expenses and income from the bank in the system is not checked. The name of the bank and the account number must be mentioned below and explained in full. Mobile banking and other things related to the bank account, if any, should be mentioned, should be written in detail.

The representative of the car is the owner of the property.

If an individual owns a shared car with other individuals and the other individuals want to give him full power of attorney, we will not include his name on the proxy, even though his name is mentioned on the license.
